The Philosophy

Just pure tools.

Tools should be fast, private, and accessible. Built on a stateless architecture, CosmoTools processes everything directly in your browser without external servers.

No Intrusive Ads

CosmoTools believe tools should solve problems, not serve advertisements. Enjoy a clean environment.

Distraction-free.

No Tracking

No analytics trackers or cookies for profiling. Your behavior and usage remain anonymous.

100% Anonymous.

No Data Collection

No accounts required. CosmoTools don't ask for your email or harvest personal information.

No logins needed.

No Backend

Files are processed locally. By eliminating the backend, CosmoTools remove the risk of breaches.

Stateless architecture.
