Privacy Philosophy

CosmoTools is designed around a simple principle: useful tools should respect user privacy. We avoid tracking, advertisements, and server-side processing.

  • _ No intrusive ads
  • _ No tracking scripts
  • _ No data collection
  • _ Local device processing
